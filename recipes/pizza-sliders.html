Pizza Sliders

These sliders really taste like pizza (as they should) but with a hint of sweetness from the Hawaiian rolls. Trying to separate them can get messy but they are absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • shredded Italian cheese blend
  • finely chopped fresh basil
  • black pepper
  • dried italian seasoning
  • pizza sauce
  • sun-dried tomato pesto
  • Hawaiian sweet rolls
  • thinly sliced pepperoni
  • butter
  • parmesan cheese
  • garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Stir mozzarella, Italian cheese blend, basil, pepper, and Italian seasoning
  3. Stir together pizza sauce and pesto
  4. Cut sweet rolls in half
  5. Sprinkle cheese blend on bottom of rolls
  6. Bake in oven until cheese melts
  7. Combine butter, parmesan, garlic powder, and italian seasoning
  8. Remove rolls and add butter mixture
