Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Chop Onion
  3. Mince Garlic
  4. Put Beef in a pan
  5. Add the Tomatos
  6. Add the Onions
  7. Add Sugar
  8. Add Garlic
  9. Assemble Lasagna
  10. Bake
